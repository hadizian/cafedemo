FAQ’S

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you have any special promotions or loyalty programs?

Yes, we offer special promotions throughout the year, including holiday discounts, limited-time menu items, and seasonal offers. We also have a loyalty program where you can earn points with each visit, which can be redeemed for discounts or exclusive rewards.

Is there parking available for guests visiting your Cafe?

Yes, we offer convenient parking for all our guests. There is ample parking available right next to the Cafe, including both free and paid parking options. Additionally, we provide valet services during peak hours for your convenience.

Do you offer catering services for private events or parties?

Yes, we offer catering services for private events, parties, and special occasions. Our team can tailor the menu to fit your specific needs, whether it's a small gathering or large celebration. Contact us in advance to plan your event and ensure a seamless experience.

What are the Cafe's hours of operation and availability?

Our Cafe is open daily from 11:00 AM to 10:00 PM. We are available for lunch, dinner, and takeout throughout these hours. For special events or holidays, our hours may vary, so we recommend checking our website or calling ahead for the most up-to-date information.

Are pets allowed in your Cafe's outdoor seating area?

Yes, pets are welcome in our outdoor seating area! We love having your furry companions join you for a meal. Please ensure they are on a leash and under control at all times. Our team will be happy to assist you and your pet during your visit.
